## Data and code availability

All source code, panels, documentation, three starter `.vcv`
patches, and the Python patch generator are available under
GPL-3.0 at:

> <https://github.com/kevinschoenholzer/empiria>

The five plugins (Polis, Methods, Epi, Space, Decisions) are
also bundled into VCV Rack `.vcvplugin` archives in the same
repository for direct installation. A submission to the VCV Rack
Library is in progress (see `SUBMISSION.md` in the repository for
the cross-platform binary distribution checklist).

## Ethical statement

The work does not involve human subjects, animal subjects, or
secondary analysis of personal data. The synthetic-data examples
in the paper are generated by deterministic seeded simulators
within Empiria itself.
