# Suggested reviewers

> **Note to author.** These are *candidate* names derived from the
> intersection of the paper's substantive scope (stats education,
> simulation-based inference, data-science pedagogy, computational
> social science) and the citation network of the manuscript. Before
> entering them into ScholarOne, please (a) confirm each candidate
> has no recent collaboration / institutional COI with you; (b)
> verify current institutional email and that they are still active;
> (c) decide whether to keep all five or substitute names from your
> own academic network. JSDSE editorial office is *not* obliged to
> use these suggestions — they are advisory only.

**Robert Gould**
Vice Chair for Undergraduate Studies

rgould@stat.ucla.edu
